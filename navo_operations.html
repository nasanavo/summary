6. NAVO Operations: Registry, VO Monitoring and Usage of NAVO interfaces

Registry Operations

MAST operates the NAVO VO registry. This is a database which stores information about VO services published worldwide. The registry is used by many VO-aware tools but can be queried manually using the Directory service. Just enter keywords describing the data of interest.

Monitoring and Validation

The HEASARC runs services which monitor and validate the operation of VO services worldwide.

Every site is tested on an hourly cadence and checked to make sure that a sensible response is received. The goal of the monitoring activity is to see if the site is up, not to do a detailed quality check. Monitoring checks are lightweight and done only on representative services to ensure minimal loading on the remote sites. Web pages are available to look at the current status of services or to get the average uptime for a time range. When services fail, NAVO personnel send messages to the responsible parties.

Validation is a more intensive check of the conformance of services to VO standards. About 3% of services are validated each day so that all services are validated over the course of a month. The results of validation are available through the validation website which allows users to drill down to the validation status of a particular service, or to look at the issues with a site or protocol overall.
